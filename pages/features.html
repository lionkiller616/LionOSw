The Anatomy of Innovation.

LionOS isn't just an operating system; it's a meticulously crafted experience. We've rethought every layer, from the desktop to the kernel, to deliver unparalleled performance, intuitive design, and intelligent features that anticipate your needs.

Desktop Experience Redefined

Lion Desktop Environment & LICO Compositor

Immerse yourself in LDE, a stunning desktop inspired by macOS 16, where elegance meets efficiency. Powered by the LICO Compositor, experience RAM-GPU accelerated graphics, silky-smooth animations, and ultra-low latency for a truly fluid interactive feel.

  • layersPixel-perfect, minimalist UI with refined typography.
  • animationButtery-smooth transitions and window effects.
  • blur_onAdaptive glassmorphism and sophisticated visual depth.
Next-Generation Storage

GhostFS: Secure, Encrypted, Ephemeral.

Introducing GhostFS, a revolutionary hybrid filesystem. It intelligently fuses concepts from ZFS, LUKS, and tmpfs to offer live on-the-fly encryption, efficient compression, and RAM-backed zero-trace temporary storage for unparalleled privacy and performance.

  • enhanced_encryptionLive, transparent data encryption for system and user files.
  • speedRAM-backed ephemeral storage for blazing-fast temporary operations.
  • shield_lockGhostVault for ultra-secure, isolated storage of sensitive data.
Intelligent & Adaptive

Proactive System Intelligence

LionOS features sophisticated offline modules that simulate AI-like behaviors. Experience a system that learns your habits for adaptive performance, offers smart app suggestions, and even attempts self-healing for common issues, all while respecting your privacy.

  • auto_awesomeAdaptive Performance: Optimizes resource allocation based on your usage.
  • healingSystem Self-Healing: Automatic patching and self-diagnosis.
  • lightbulbSmart Suggestions: Contextual app and action recommendations (offline).

Beyond the highlights, LionOS is packed with features that refine every aspect of your computing.
