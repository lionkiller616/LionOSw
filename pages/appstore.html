A Glimpse into the Future

While the full LionOS App Center is under active development, here's a preview of our vision for a seamless and secure application experience.

Key Design Principles

  • verified

    Curated & Trusted

    Access a carefully selected library of high-quality applications, reviewed for security, stability, and a great user experience.

  • palette

    Beautifully Simple Interface

    An intuitive, visually appealing design that makes finding and installing apps effortless and enjoyable, inspired by the LionOS aesthetic.

  • security

    Enhanced Security & Permissions

    Clear information about app permissions, sandboxing (where applicable), and easy management of what your apps can access.

  • apps

    Seamless Integration

    Applications designed or adapted for LionOS, ensuring they look, feel, and perform beautifully within the LDE.

  • update

    Effortless Updates

    Keep your applications up-to-date with a straightforward and reliable update mechanism, integrated with the system updater.

  • developer_mode

    Developer Friendly

    Easy submission and management process for developers looking to bring their applications to the LionOS ecosystem.
